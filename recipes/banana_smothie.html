BANANA SMOTHIE

Description:

Banana shakes can be made to benefit weight loss or gain, a fitness routine, vegan diet, or low carb lifestyle. You can enjoy them as a snack, quick and easy breakfast, or even as a dessert option.

Unlike juicing, blending can incorporate all of the edible parts of fruits and vegetables â€” including the skin, seeds and pulp so a smoothie can be nutritionally very similar to eating the produce whole.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Bananas
  • 350 ml Fresh Milk
  • 6 gr Sugar
  • 50 gr Greek Yogurt
  • 2 Ice Blocks

Steps

  1. Slice the banana and put it in a bowl.
  2. Add the milk and blend it all together.
  3. Add the sugar and mix it.
  4. Now that everything is well mixed togehter put it in a glass and its almost ready.
  5. Add the ice you want and enjoy a delicious and healthy banana smothie.
