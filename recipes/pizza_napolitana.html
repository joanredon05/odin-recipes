PIZZA NAPOLITANA

Description:

Neapolitan pizza is a style of pizza made with tomatoes and mozzarella cheese.The tomatoes must be either San Marzano tomatoes or pomodorino del Piennolo del Vesuvio, which grow on the volcanic plains to the south of Mount Vesuvius. The cheese must be mozzarella di bufala campana, or fior di latte.

Pizza napoletana is a TSG product in the European Union and the United Kingdom, and the art of its making is included on UNESCO's list of intangible cultural heritage.

Ingredients:

  • 570 gr Flour Tipo 00
  • 4 gr Fresh yeast
  • 350 ml Water
  • 6 gr Sugar
  • 17 ml Olive oil
  • 11 gr Salt

Steps

  1. Put the water in a bowl and mix it with the salt.
  2. Add 10% of flour to the mix and add the fresh yeast, beat it until the yeast is disolved in the dought
  3. Add the sugar and mix it.
  4. Add all the left flour and beat and knead the dought until forms a consistent texture.
  5. Add the oil and do the same again, then remove it from the bowl and knead for 20 min.
  6. Once the dought has a consistent texture put it again in the sealed bowl and let it rest for 2 hours.
  7. The size of the dought should have increased, now cut the dought in to 4 pieces and round them.
  8. Put the small portions in different sealed containers let them rest for at least 6 hours and they will be ready for an awesome homemade pizza napolitana
